MISSION

Our mission is to use digital literacy classes to transform kids into self-motivated learners who are curious, inventive & resourceful.
Most importantly, we partner with families to provide children with a safe and encouraging environment to gain the skills they need for a brighter future. We look forward to earning your trust.
Coder Kids express their creativity with digital tools, learn computing skills they can apply everyday, and are inspired with a passion for technology that brings advantages for life.
In creating apps, games, and websites with their own code and at their own pace, CodeCamp learn strategies for analyzing, designing, and collaborating — essential skills for the 21st century.Our mission at CodeCamp is to provide an experience that far exceeds playing video games or taking one-time or short-term courses.

GOALS

We aim to create an engaging activity camp where programming knowledge is acquired alongside 21st century skills through collaboratively playing and solving puzzles, inventing, creating and sharing.
Kids love technology, games, and having fun with friends. Parents want to give their kids the best and brightest future possible. Everyone wins at CodeCamp. Kids learn to code through our engaging game-based curriculum that teaches teamwork, logic, math, and problem solving. They gain vital skills while having a blast.
Technology is advancing at an incredible rate, and we're just getting started. Code Ninjas believes that there's never been a more exciting time to advance our culture - starting with our youngest ones. Every kid deserves the chance to unlock their innate, unlimited capacity to learn. Whether or not they pursue coding as a career, we want to provide kids with skills that will help them pursue their dreams.

HISTORY

Code Camp was founded in 2014, based on Jonathan’s successful experiences in teaching young children to code through playful activities. Today, at Code Camp, we are bringing fun, knowledge and opportunity into the lives of thousands of learners around the world.
CodeCamp was born out of a desire to fill a need in education, a need to provide a true, comprehensive technology education in a fun, stress-free environment. Our STEM focused courses and tech camps provide the basis for a much deeper education in technology than what students receive at their schools. This is because our instructors are all both industry tested STEM and computer programming professionals, and because these instructors have gained a wealth of teaching experience to kids in STEM, Technology, and Coding in various formats.

SAFETY & PROTECTION

Every instructor and staff member goes through a series of Computer literacy tests, fingerprinting, and full child safety training. We are also first aid trained.
We pride ourselves in partnering with entrepreneurs to bring 21st century learning centers to communities across the country. Most importantly, we partner with families to provide children with a safe and encouraging environment to gain the skills they need for a brighter future.
We look forward to earning your trust.
At CodeCamp we are passionate about community. CodeCamp publish their work on the CodeCamp community forum where they can collaborate with the peer coders and give and receive feedback. At the completion of a course, CodeCamp are invited to celebrate their work and build fellowship with their parents, friends, family, and CodeCamp at our quarterly CodeCamp SEED events.

RESULTS
