MISSION

Our mission is to use digital literacy classes to transform kids into self-motivated learners who are curious, inventive & resourceful.

GOALS

We aim to create an engaging activity camp where programming knowledge is acquired alongside 21st century skills through collaboratively playing and solving puzzles, inventing, creating and sharing.

VALUES

We promote good citizenship to our Kids and teach them to use their skills for good and to share their knowledge with others. We ask Kids to participate in anti-bullying activities and teach kids and teens how to deal with difficult scenarios. We work to empower kids and arm them with tools that benefit them for the rest of their lives.

HISTORY

Code Camp was founded in 2014, based on Jonathan’s successful experiences in teaching young children to code through playful activities. Today, at Code Camp, we are bringing fun, knowledge and opportunity into the lives of thousands of learners around the world.

SAFETY & PROTECTION

Every instructor and staff member goes through a series of Computer literacy tests, fingerprinting, and full child safety training. We are also first aid trained.

RESULTS

Kids will learn about coding, design, being a maker, science projects, play with virtual reality and so many amazing opportunities to be a creator!
